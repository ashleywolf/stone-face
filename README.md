# 🗿 Stone Face

Keep a straight face for 60 seconds while the game throws provocations at you. AI tracks 52 facial expression blendshapes. Any smile, surprise, or eyebrow raise drains your composure. The audience tries to make you crack.

## How to Play

Open in Chrome and allow webcam access. Calibrate your neutral face. Survive 60 seconds of escalating provocations — emoji, text jokes, distortion filters, sound effects. Any expression drains your composure meter.

## Tech

MediaPipe FaceLandmarker tracking 52 blendshapes including mouthSmile, jawOpen, browOuterUp, eyeSquint, and cheekPuff. HTML5 Canvas. Single file, CDN-loaded.

## Input

Webcam (expression suppression — the only game where doing NOTHING is the goal)
